Getting to Ronda

By Car

Ronda is easily accessible by car from many other Andalusian towns. From towns on the Costa del Sol such as Marbella, Ronda is only a one hour drive, taking in beautiful scenery and dramatic landscapes along the way. Ronda is also an easy drive from nearby cities such as Malaga and Seville.

Marbella to Ronda: 1 hour (60km)

Malaga to Ronda: 1 hour 20 minutes (100km)

Seville to Ronda: 1 hour 40 minutes (130km)

Cordoba: 2 hours 10 minutes (160km)

Street parking in the old town area of Ronda is only allowed for residents. Several large car parks are available for parking such as in Plaza del Socorro (underground car park), La Concepción (close to the bus station) and El Castillo (close to Plaza Duquesa de Parcent).

By Train

Ronda is easily accessible from other Andalusian towns by train and the routes feature beautiful scenery.

Train tickets can be booked using the Renfe website.

Map of the train routes of Andalusia

By bus

Ronda is easily accessible by bus from many other towns in Andalusia.

Bus companies:

Comes: www.tgcomes.es/

Avanzabus: malaga.avanzagrupo.com

Damas: damas-sa.es

Paco Pepe: www.grupopacopepe.com

By Plane

Andalusia has 2 international airports, Seville and Malaga, with both offering connections to Ronda by bus.

Damas offers routes between Ronda and Malaga/Seville: https://damas-sa.es/

Avanzabus offers a service between Malaga and Ronda: https://www.avanzabus.com/

Also in Andalusia there are 4 domestic airports: Almería, Cordoba, Granada-Jaén and Jerez de la Frontera.
