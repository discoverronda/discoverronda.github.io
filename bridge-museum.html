Bridge Museum

The interpretation centre provides some basic information about the bridge, including its construction. The most impressive is the location of the interpretation centre, which is inside a chamber above the central arch of the bridge. The area around the interpretation centre also provides incredible views of the gorge and buildings around the bridge.
