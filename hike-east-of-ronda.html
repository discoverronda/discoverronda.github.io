East of Ronda walk

Starting at the Puerta de Almocábar on the edge of the old town of Ronda, this walk will take you east towards the valleys around Ronda and will give you the opportunity to see some incredible panoramic vistas of the city from a distance. After that the walk will take you back towards the new town of Ronda and will leave you at the edge of the main thoroughfare, Calle Espinel.
