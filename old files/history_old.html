What makes Ronda special?

My favorite activity to look forward to is always when I can get on the train and go travelling - my holidays, weekends and every amount of time away from work were always the times I was looking forward to the most. They were like those treats for me for which I was always looking forward to and couldn’t wait to start. Why? Unfortunately I do not have very rational or logical answers to it - it just feels right and every time it happens it makes my heart grow and I come back from every trip feeling a little bit better with more knowledge and with more love and appreciation for the world. I am the type of traveller who likes to wander, learn and also relax during a trip.

My favorite places to visit are smaller towns where I can explore streets or just relax in a coffee shop, order something simple and enjoy watching the world goes by. I also enjoy spending time close to nature, walking in forests, hiking, visiting national parks or just finding new places and following where the map takes me. I like that mix of travel while being always in close proximity to nature, it gives me that feeling of excitement of exploring and being close to nature makes me feel complete. I also enjoy visiting countries I have never visited bforebut at the same time I also enjoy coming back to the same places I visited before. It is enjoyable and educational seeing how they change over a time. They become more like my other homes which I get to understand better with every minute spent there.

The Serrania

As my travels are my treats, I was always trying to choose the right accommodation which fits perfectly with what I need. Due to money constraints I usually compromised on some things I was looking for in accommodation but over time I decided that since it is my treat time I want it to be as free from concerns as possible so I started to look for more expensive accommodation hoping that it will tick all the boxes and at the same time I tried to save on other things.

I don’t mind paying more for accommodation if I know it will provide what I need but unfortunately over time I noticed that it is not actually always happening. Not always but unfortunately quite often I came back from my trips with a few disappointing memories of my accommodation. These were not a big things, these were actually very easily avoidable or fixable issues but unfortunately either my requests were forgotten or badly handled or even sometimes just ignored.

I would usually spend a good deal of time on choosing the right accommodation as it was a place where I would not only like to come back and have a good nights sleep but also relax while drinking a cup of tea or just sitting on the terrace or just popping in for a break from my sightseeing for a few minutes and relaxing before getting back to my adventures.
