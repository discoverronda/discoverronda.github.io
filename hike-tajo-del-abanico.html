Tajo del Abanico

The Tajo del Abanico is a relatively easy walk to a gorge outside of Ronda. The walk takes you initially through the Barrio of San Francisco and after that along trails surrounded by olive groves, and eventually to higher ground and beautiful scenery on the way to the Tajo del Abanico.
