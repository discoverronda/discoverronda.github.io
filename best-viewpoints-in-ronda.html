Visit Ronda: 10 must-stop viewpoints

Sitting on top of its famous gorge, Ronda literally takes your breath away when you see it for the first time. This beautiful and romantic town of Spain, with its charming narrow winding streets and white buildings, has been an inspiration for many writers and artists in the past. Hemingway said that Ronda “is where you should go if you ever go to Spain on a honeymoon or if you ever bolt with anyone. The entire town and as far as you can see in any direction is romantic background.” Do you need other reasons to visit Ronda?

This town is the perfect size for a day-trip, but it is also perfect if you want to spend more days hiking around the gorge or just relaxing drinking wine with an amazing view.

1. Ronda Bridge View Point

The best place to admire Ronda is “Ronda Bridge View Point, from which you can see the town and the most famous bridge of Spain from the bottom of the gorge. This is also the perfect place to start and end your day in Ronda, especially if you are by car (read until the end of the post to find out where to park your car).
