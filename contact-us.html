About Me

I'm David and Discover Ronda is my passion project, a place for people to get to know Ronda a little better and to share my passion for photography and website creation, having created this website entirely from scratch without the use of templates or website builders. Originally from Ireland, I visited Ronda numerous times in the past and now am delighted to live here permanently and be able to call Ronda home.
