El Tajo Gorge Loop

Starting at the Plaza del Toros bullring, this hike will take you to the northside of Ronda and down a trail that leads into the valleys around the gorge with incredible scenery and peaceful trails throughout. The walk eventually brings you back towards the direction of the old town of Ronda, but not before enjoying some views of the Puente Nuevo as you climb back towards the old town.
