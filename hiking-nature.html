Bird Watching

The Serranía de Ronda offers a diverse and unique habitat for a huge variety of birds, from smaller birds like goldfinches, stonechats and kingfishers to larger birds such as kestrels, peregrine falcons and griffin vultures. Also the natural parks that surround Ronda such as The Sierra de las Nieves and Sierra de Grazalema offer an ideal place to go birdwatching.

Horseback Riding

Several horseback riding companies in Ronda offer riding tours.

Cycling

Ronda offers many cycling routes with its ideal location in the heart of the Serrania de Ronda. Routes such as Ronda to Arriate and Ronda to El Burgo make for a great cycling trip outside of the town. There are also bike rental locations in Ronda.

Flying

Hot air balloon flights, paragliding and more are activities to enjoy while in Ronda.
