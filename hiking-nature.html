Bird Watching

The Serranía de Ronda offers a diverse and unique habitat for a huge variety of birds, from smaller birds like goldfinches, stonechats and kingfishers to larger birds such as kestrels, peregrine falcons and griffin vultures. Also the natural parks that surround Ronda such as The Sierra de las Nieves and Sierra de Grazalema offer an ideal place to go birdwatching.
