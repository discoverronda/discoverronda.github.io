Paseo de Blas Infante

A beautiful park with small gardens and a bandstand and viewpoint locally known as the balcon del “coño”, which is the word that Spanish people normally exclaim when they see just how far down the gorge drops below the viewpoint. The park is also home to many wild cats, so no matter how few people are in the park, you can always feel eyes watching you as you walk through the park!
