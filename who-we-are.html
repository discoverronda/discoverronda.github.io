Why I Travel:

My favorite activity to look forward to is always when I can get on the train and go travelling - my holidays, weekends and every amount of time away from work were always the times I was looking forward to the most. They were like those treats for me for which I was always looking forward to and couldn’t wait to start. Why? Unfortunately I do not have very rational or logical answers to it - it just feels right and every time it happens it makes my hearts grow and I come back from every trip feeling a little bit better with more knowledge and with more love and appreciation for the world. I am the type of traveller who likes to wander, learn and also relax during a trip.

My favorite places to visit are smaller towns where I can explore streets or just relax in a coffee shop, order something small and enjoy watching the world goes by. I also enjoy spending time close to nature, walking in forests, hiking, visiting national parks or just finding new places and following where the map takes me. I enjoy new places or countries from time to time but at the same time I also enjoy coming back to the same places I visited before. It is enjoyable and educational seeing how they change over a time. They become more like my other homes which I get to understand better with every minute spent there.

Accommodation I choose:

As my travels are my treats, I was always trying to choose the right accommodation which fits perfectly with what I need. Due to money constraints I usually compromised on some things I was looking for in accommodation but over time I decided that since it is my treat time I want it to be as free from concerns as possible so I started to look for more expensive accommodation hoping that it will tick all the boxes and at the same time I tried to save on other things.

I don’t mind paying more for accommodation if I know it will provide what I need but unfortunately over time I noticed that it is not actually always happening. Not always but unfortunately quite often I came back from my trips with a few disappointing memories of my accommodation. These were not a big things, these were actually very easily avoidable or fixable issues but unfortunately either my requests were forgotten or badly handled or even sometimes just ignored.

I would usually spend a good deal of time on choosing the right accommodation as it was a place where I would not only like to come back and have a good nights sleep but also relax while drinking a cup of tea or just sitting on the terrace or just popping in for a break from my sightseeing for a few minutes and relaxing before getting back to my adventures.

My dream B & B:

It was not easy for me to find an accommodation that would tick all the boxes, I was ready to pay more but at the same I would not like it to ruin me financially. So I was always saying that some day I will create my own B&B where I can offer the type of service that I have been always looking for. Additionally, I has been working for many years in different hotels learning how to provide best guest service possible...and that’s how it all started...
