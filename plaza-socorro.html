Plaza del Socorro

Situated in the centre of the New town of Ronda, the Plaza del Socorro is one of the most iconic areas of Ronda, with the church of Socorro of Ronda at the centre and a variety of bars and restaurants around the square that give it a lively and friendly atmosphere. Sitio web
